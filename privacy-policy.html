Privacy Policy - Persona Spectrum

Last updated: August 2025

Welcome to Persona Spectrum. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register, we collect:

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)

1.2 Assessment Data

To provide your personality analysis, we store:

  • Your answers to the assessment questions.
  • The calculated personality scores and recommendations.
  • Timestamps of when assessments were taken.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Allow login and secure your account.
  • Generate your personalized personality profile and development plan.
  • Display your assessment history.
  • Enhance app performance and fix technical issues.

3. Data Security

We take measures to secure your data, including encrypted password storage and secure communication between the app and our server.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • View your profile and assessment history at any time.
  • Request account and data deletion.

5. Third-Party Services

We do not share your personal data with any third-party services for marketing or advertising purposes.

6. Children's Privacy

Persona Spectrum is not intended for children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of any significant changes.
